# M15 — AuroraImage dispatch invocation contract centralization (image_dispatch.py)

**Timeframe:** Initialized 2026-03-29; merged to `main` 2026-03-29 (PR #16, merge `4d694b3…`).

## Summary

M15 added **`image_dispatch.py`** as the **single owner** of the **`Dispatcher.dispatch`** invocation pattern: **`(token, payload, lib)`** after acquiring the shared library handle. **`image.py`** keeps **`ImageCreationError`** wrapping and **`AuroraImage`** construction.

## What changed

- `dispatch_image_from_file`, `dispatch_image_from_bytes`, tests for ordering and tokens.

## What it proved

- The **call shape** at the Python seam is centralized and test-pinned.
- Domain module stays thin — dispatch details live in one internal module.

## What it did not prove

- Native behavior or decode.

## Key deliverables

- `image_dispatch.py` as internal API.

## Evidence (public)

- PR #16; CI success.

## Why it mattered

Audio dispatch (M19) could mirror the same **frozen arity** pattern (`audio_dispatch.py`).
